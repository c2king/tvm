Time Value of Money Quiz

Introduction

Time Value of Money (TVM) is a fundamental concept in finance that recognizes that money available now is worth more than the same amount in the future due to its potential earning capacity. This concept is crucial for university business students to understand because it forms the basis for many financial decisions, including investment analysis, capital budgeting, and loan calculations.

Key TVM concepts include:

  • Present Value (PV)
  • Future Value (FV)
  • Compound Interest
  • Annuities

Understanding TVM allows students to make informed financial decisions and evaluate investment opportunities effectively.

How to Play

  1. Click the "Start Quiz" button to begin.
  2. Read each question carefully and select the best answer from the four options provided.
  3. If you answer correctly, you can move to the next question.
  4. If you answer incorrectly, you'll have another chance to select the correct answer.
  5. Use the "Next" and "Previous" buttons to navigate through the questions.
  6. Answer all 10 questions to complete the quiz.
